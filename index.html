About

I'm a Licensed Civil Engineer with over five years of combined experience across customer support, sales, appointment setting, and construction engineering. Most of that time has been spent working with U.S.-based clients in remote settings — handling high volumes of conversations, qualifying leads, and helping people find the right fit.

In one stretch I managed more than 100 customer conversations a day and closed 55 sales in 15 days, on nothing but consistent follow-up and clear, honest communication. I like the two sides of this work for the same reason: both reward precision, patience, and actually listening to what's in front of you.
